I am a PhD candidate and research assistant at the Institute for Computer Graphics and Vision at the Graz University of Technology. My primary research interests are Information Visualization, Bioinformatics and Human Computer Interaction. I am working on the Caleydo project where we visualize biomolecular data to help life science experts to understand the function of genes.
